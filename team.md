# Team

*Meet the people building the future of distributed computing.*

<br>

## Core Team

### Leadership

The AetherGrid project is led by a team of experienced engineers, researchers, and blockchain enthusiasts committed to democratizing access to computational resources.

**Founders**
- Distributed computing experts with backgrounds in supercomputing and blockchain technology
- Combined 30+ years of experience in distributed systems
- Published researchers in peer-to-peer networking and cryptography

### Development Team

Our development team includes:

- **Systems Engineers**: Building the core distributed computing infrastructure
- **Blockchain Developers**: Maintaining the AetherGrid blockchain and token economics
- **Security Researchers**: Ensuring cryptographic security and privacy
- **DevOps Engineers**: Managing network infrastructure and reliability
- **Frontend Developers**: Creating user-friendly interfaces and tools

### Research & Innovation

- **Computer Science Researchers**: Advancing distributed computing algorithms
- **Cryptography Experts**: Developing secure verification mechanisms
- **Network Scientists**: Optimizing peer-to-peer protocols
- **Economics Advisors**: Designing sustainable token economics
