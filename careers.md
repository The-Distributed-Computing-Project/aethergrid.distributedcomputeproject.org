# Careers

*Join us in building the future of distributed computing.*

<br>

## Why AetherGrid?

### Our Mission

We're building a decentralized computing platform that makes high-performance computing accessible to everyone. Join us in democratizing computational resources and creating a more efficient, sustainable approach to distributed computing.

### Our Values

- **Open Source First**: We believe in transparent, community-driven development
- **Innovation**: Pushing the boundaries of distributed computing and blockchain technology
- **Sustainability**: Building energy-efficient solutions for global computational needs
- **Inclusivity**: Creating opportunities for contributors worldwide

### What We Offer

- Work on cutting-edge distributed systems and blockchain technology
- Collaborate with a global team of talented engineers and researchers
- Flexible, remote-friendly work environment
- Contribute to open-source software with real-world impact
- Competitive compensation in tokens and/or traditional currency

## How to Apply

### Application Process

1. **Submit Application**: Send your resume and cover letter to careers@aethergrid.org
2. **Initial Screening**: We'll review your application within 1-2 weeks
3. **Technical Interview**: Discuss your experience and problem-solving approach
4. **Project Evaluation**: Complete a relevant technical challenge or code review
5. **Team Interview**: Meet with team members and discuss cultural fit
6. **Offer**: Receive offer with compensation details

### What to Include

- **Resume/CV**: Highlighting relevant experience
- **Cover Letter**: Explaining your interest in AetherGrid and the role
- **Portfolio**: Links to GitHub, projects, or publications
- **References**: Professional references (optional for initial application)
